Amrai Pari Open Scout Group is a Scouting organization of Bangladesh. It was established in 2019. The organization aims to develop the physical, mental, and spiritual capacities of young people in Bangladesh, helping them become responsible citizens and leaders in their communities. It operates through a network of local Scout groups, known as "troops," which are organized at the community level. Amrai Pari Open Scout Group organizes various programs and activities for young people, including camping, hiking, community service, and leadership training. It also participates in international Scouting events and exchanges with other Scouting organizations around the world. Scouts have been trained in first aid, search and rescue, and emergency response, and have played an active role in responding to natural disasters such as floods, cyclones, and earthquakes. Overall, the Amrai Pari Open Scout is an important organization in the country, providing opportunities for young people to develop important life skills and make a positive impact on their communities.
